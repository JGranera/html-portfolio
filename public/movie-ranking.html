The Best Movies According to Jorge

The Top Three Movies That Always Make Me Cry

Big Fish

This is my all time favorite movie. It tackles a lot of topics I personally relate to. There is the storyteller aspect. The father/son dynamic and the hopeful optimism that being a good person can get you far in life. I cry when I get to the end and Will and his father are able to come to an understanding about each other. It is a perfect film.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

A powerful story that honestly is better than it needed to be. Truthfully, if the tone would've stayed the way it was during the first two acts I think it would've been a classic action movie. But when it dives into the lives of the characters and the hurt they are experiencing it takes this film to a level that made it an instant classic. I cry everytime Evelyn realizes how much she loves Waymond and how he is her connection to seeing the good this existence has to offer.

Cinema Paradiso

A simple, yet powerful story. This is a filmmaker's love letter to film and the places we come from. I can't quite articulate what it is about this movie that I find so sad. I think it might be because its about the passage of time and how the magic we experience as a kid changes as we get older. The memories are forever there but that time has passed and we can never go back and connect to it in the same way. This is a film that will always hold a special place in my heart.
